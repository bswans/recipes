Butternut Squash Stew (vegetarian):

Ingerdients:
	1 butternut squash (peeled & cut into 1/2" cubes)
	3 1/2 c vegetable broth
	1 tbsp olive oil
	1 yellow onion
	1/2 tsp salt
	4 cloves garlic
	1 1/2 tsp dried oregano
	1 (14 oz) can diced tomatoes
	2/3 c uncooked quinoa
	1 (15 oz) can chickpeas
	1 (15 oz) can black beans
	~ 6 c water to steam squash

Directions:
	Steam butternut squash for ~10 minutes, until barely tender.
		Remove half the pieces and set aside.
	Steam rest of butternut squash pieces another ~4-6 min.
	 	Mash remaining pieces with fork.
	Place a large saucepan on medium heat. 
		Add olive oil & onion ~8-10 min until browning.
		Add salt, garlic, and oregano.
		 	Stir 1 min. 	
		Add tomatoes & butternut squash (pieces and mashed) and stir.
		Stir in broth and quinoa.
	Simmer for ~15 minutes until quinoa is translucent.
		With 5 or so minutes left add chick peas and black beans.
